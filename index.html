Asteroids in Galactica

Lost in Galactica the spacecraft SC Chimera has encountered an overwhelming field of asteroids. As captain of the ship it is your duty to destroy all incoming asteroids and navigate your crew to safety.

Navigation & Shooting

The ship starts out with 5 health points, HP decreases on impact with asteroids

If you collide with an asteroid you can evade more damage by navigating away

Use the left and right arrow keys to evade incoming asteriods

Use the space button to shoot your lazer and destroy the asteroid
